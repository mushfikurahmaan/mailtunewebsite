Refund Policy

Last Updated: June 15, 2023

1. Overview

At Mail Tune, this Refund Policy outlines our guidelines for cancellations of subscriptions to the Mail Tune service. Please note that we do not provide money-back refunds for subscriptions.

By subscribing to Mail Tune, you agree to the terms of this Refund Policy. If you do not agree with these terms, please do not purchase a subscription.

2. No-Refund Policy

All purchases made for Mail Tune subscriptions are final and non-refundable. We do not provide refunds for any subscription payments already processed.

We encourage all users to:

  • Try our free plan before committing to a paid subscription
  • Review all features and limitations of each plan before purchasing
  • Contact our support team with any questions before subscribing

3. Subscription Cancellations

You may cancel your subscription at any time through your account settings. Upon cancellation:

  • Your subscription will remain active until the end of your current billing period
  • You will not be charged for the next billing period
  • No partial refunds will be issued for unused time in the current billing period
  • You will lose access to premium features when your current billing period ends

4. Technical Issues

If you experience technical issues with our service that substantially impact your ability to use Mail Tune, please contact our support team. We are committed to resolving any technical problems you may encounter.

If we are unable to resolve the issue within a reasonable timeframe, we may extend your subscription period as a courtesy, depending on the circumstances.

5. Business and Enterprise Plans

For Business and Enterprise plans, special terms may apply. Please contact our sales team at sales@mailtune.com to discuss your specific situation.

6. Changes to Refund Policy

We reserve the right to modify this Refund Policy at any time. Any changes will be effective immediately upon posting on our website. Your continued use of our Service after such changes constitutes your acceptance of the new Refund Policy.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about our Refund Policy, please contact us at support@mailtune.com.
