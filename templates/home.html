What is tone in mail?

Email tone is the attitude and emotion conveyed in your written communication. It significantly impacts how your message is perceived and can make the difference between effective and ineffective communication.

The right tone ensures your message is received as intended, whether you're expressing urgency, showing appreciation, or requesting information. It helps build relationships, avoid misunderstandings, and achieve your communication goals.

It's not just what you say, but how you say it.

Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about Mail Tune.

How does Mail Tune work?

Mail Tune works as a browser extension that seamlessly integrates with your email client. Just install our extension, compose your email as usual, select your desired tone, and our AI will instantly improve your message—all without leaving your email platform.

Is my email data secure?

Absolutely. We never store your emails after processing. Your data is encrypted during transmission and we operate with strict privacy controls in compliance with GDPR regulations.

What tone options are available?

Mail Tune offers multiple tones including Professional, Friendly, Direct, Apologetic, Persuasive, and more. Premium plans allow for custom tone creation tailored to your specific communication needs.

How many emails can I tune?

This depends on your plan. Free users get 5 email transformations, Starter provides 100 emails per month, Premium offers 500 emails monthly, and Business users get unlimited emails (subject to fair use policy).

Which browsers and email clients are supported?

Our extension works with Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Brave browsers. It's compatible with Gmail, Outlook, Yahoo Mail, and most web-based email clients. We're continuously expanding our compatibility to support more platforms.

Can I use Mail Tune for languages other than English?

Currently, Mail Tune works best with English text. We're actively working on expanding our language support to include other major languages in future updates.
