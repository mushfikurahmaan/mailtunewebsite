Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 15, 2023

1. Introduction

At Mail Tune ("we," "our," or "us"), we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our website, browser extension, and related services (collectively, the "Service").

By using our Service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our Service.

2. Information We Collect

We collect several types of information from and about users of our Service, including:

  • Personal Data: We may collect personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, and payment information when you create an account or purchase a subscription.
  • Email Content: Our Service processes the content of emails that you choose to modify through our Service. This is necessary to provide tone adjustment services.
  • Usage Data: We collect information about how you interact with our Service, including the features you use, the time spent on the Service, and other diagnostic data.
  • Device Information: We may collect information about your device, including browser type, IP address, and operating system.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for various purposes, including to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our Service
  • Process transactions and send related information, including confirmations and invoices
  • Send administrative messages, such as updates, security alerts, and support messages
  • Monitor usage patterns and analyze trends to improve user experience
  • Address technical issues and troubleshoot problems
  • Protect against fraudulent, unauthorized, or illegal activity

4. Email Content Processing

Our primary service involves processing the content of your emails to adjust tone. Here's how we handle this data:

  • We process email content solely for the purpose of providing the tone adjustment service.
  • We do not store your email content after processing is complete.
  • Email content is encrypted during transmission to and from our servers.
  • We do not use your email content to target advertising or for any purpose other than providing and improving our service.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your information in the following situations:

  • Service Providers: We may share your information with third-party vendors, service providers, and other business partners who perform services on our behalf, such as payment processing, data analysis, and customer service.

  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.
  • Protection of Rights: We may disclose your information to protect and defend the rights, property, or safety of Mail Tune, our users, or the public.

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect the security of your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction. However, please understand that no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

We are committed to conducting our business in accordance with these principles to ensure that the confidentiality of personal information is protected and maintained.
